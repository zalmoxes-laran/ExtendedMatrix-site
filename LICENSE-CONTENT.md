## What CC BY-NC 4.0 means in practice

You are free to:

- **Share** — copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format.
- **Adapt** — remix, transform, and build upon the material.

Under the following terms:

- **Attribution** — you must give appropriate credit to "Extended Matrix
  project — extendedmatrix.org", provide a link to the licence, and
  indicate if changes were made.
- **NonCommercial** — you may not use the material for **commercial purposes**.

No additional restrictions are imposed: you may not apply legal terms or
technological measures that legally restrict others from doing anything
the licence permits.

## What is *not* covered by CC BY-NC

Several elements in this repository are **explicitly outside** this
licence:

- **Code**: Astro components, layouts, scripts and build configuration

  executable code*, `astro.config.mjs`, `tsconfig.json`, …) — released
  under **GPL-3.0-or-later**, consistent with the rest of the
  Extended Matrix Framework.
- **Third-party logos** in `src/assets/logos/` (StratiGraph, SSHOC,
  university and institutional marks, partner logos): copyright of their

If in doubt, write to
[emanuel.demetrescu@cnr.it](mailto:emanuel.demetrescu@cnr.it).

## Why CC BY-NC and not BY only

The EM project is publicly funded research, and we want the material to
flow freely for teaching, study, citation and remixing in academic and
heritage contexts. The NonCommercial clause is there to keep the
project's output out of for-profit content syndication, advertising
products and AI-training pipelines that monetise the work without
returning value to the research community.

Commercial reuse is welcome on a case-by-case basis — please reach out
to the project lead to discuss the scope of the use.
