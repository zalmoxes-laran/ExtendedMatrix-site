## What it is

**Heriverse** is the web platform where EM reconstructions become public
artefacts. A scene authored in EM Tools can be exported and opened in
Heriverse — every stratigraphic unit retains its paradata chain, every
epoch is switchable, and multiple viewers can explore the same scene
together (voice and screen sharing).

Built on the ATON 3 framework. The conceptual model behind Heriverse is
called *StratiVerse* — discussed in the scientific papers but transparent
to end users.

## What you can do

- **Browse a public scene gallery** of EM reconstructions.
- **Authenticate** via Digilab and **create your own scenes**, attach a
  knowledge graph.
- **Collaborate live** with voice chat and screen sharing.
- Enter **VR mode** with a headset for immersive exploration.

See the [Heriverse documentation](https://docs.extendedmatrix.org/projects/heriverse/en/latest/)
for the user manual.
