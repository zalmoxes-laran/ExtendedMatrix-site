## What's new in 1.5

- **Landscape mode** — manage multiple archaeological graphs in a single Blender scene.
- **CronoFilter** — chronological-horizons manager driving multi-graph scenes.
- **TSU (Transformation Stratigraphic Units)** — the conservation/restoration node type, first introduced *in nuce* in EM 1.4, is consolidated here with the full vocabulary for documenting *states* and *transformations* (decay, restoration, thematic surveys).
- **s3dgraphy 1.5.0** — first standalone release of the Python library. In EM 1.4 the code lived bundled inside EM Tools; here it becomes its own component, published on PyPI. Currently `0.1.37` in development; will be tagged **1.5.0** at release to align with the EM core line.
- **3DSC** — the 3D Survey Collection, sketched alongside EM 1.4, now matures into a fully featured Blender add-on with a companion Metashape pipeline.
- **Stratigraphy Manager** — complete rewrite (formerly US/USV Manager) with containment filters, instance chain tracking and associated documents.
- **Anastylosis Manager (RMSF)** — link 3D objects to SpecialFind nodes with LOD management.
- **3D Document Manager** — spatial-temporal document management with camera and image-plane support.
- **Graph Editor** — node-based graph visualisation in Blender's Node Editor.
- **Proxy Box Creator** — 7-point measurement tool with optional paradata enrichment.
- **Tapestry Integration** — AI-powered photorealistic reconstruction (experimental).
- Distribution as a Blender Extension (`.zip`) with automatic dependency management.
- Heriverse export functionality.

> **Heads-up:** 1.5 is the *development* line. For production projects on
> archived data, consider EM 1.4 LTS instead.
