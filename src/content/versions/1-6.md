## Look ahead

EM 1.6 is the active development line that follows the 1.5 LTS cut.
The detailed list of features in flight — multi-source assembly,
boolean + LOD surface strategies, the image viewing system, richer
georeferencing, and the next round of Author/License surfacing — is
maintained on the **[Extended Matrix development tracker](https://dev.extendedmatrix.org/dev-projects/)**.

Until 1.6 reaches a usable preview, new projects should start from
**[EM 1.5 LTS](/versions/1-5/)**.
