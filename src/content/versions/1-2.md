## What 1.2 introduced

EM 1.2 formalises the **five-step reconstructive method** that is still
the methodological backbone of every later release: from raw
stratigraphic evidence to the virtual reconstruction, with explicit
paradata at every step. The 2021 *Applied Sciences* paper (Demetrescu &
Ferdani) is the canonical reference — cite it whenever your project
relies on the five-step formalisation, regardless of the EM version you
authored under.

## Status

Legacy. Suitable for opening older projects authored in 1.2. New work
should adopt 1.4 LTS or 1.5.
