The first **public** development meeting for the Extended Matrix codebase.
Modelled after the
[blender.org module meetings](https://devtalk.blender.org/) — open by
default, notes-first, decisions visible.

**Why public?** As more people start contributing (in particular with
AI-assisted "vibe coding"), it is critical that direction is set
collaboratively before parallel forks start diverging. Decisions should
travel with their reasoning, not just with their commit.

**Standing agenda**

1. Announcements & releases since last meeting.
2. PRs and proposals on the table.
3. Architectural decisions to discuss.
4. Open floor.

**Notes** — populated after the meeting; the maintainer or a
volunteer note-taker fills in `decisions` and `actionItems` in the
frontmatter, plus a brief narrative below.
