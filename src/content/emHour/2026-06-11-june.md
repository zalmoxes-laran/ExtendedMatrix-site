The **first community happy hour** of the new EM cycle — open floor,
screen-shares welcome, no registration. This was the kickoff
session that launched the monthly cadence (2nd Thursday of each
month, 14:00 CET).

**📅 Thursday, 11 June 2026  ·  🕓 14:00 – 15:00 CET**

What was on the agenda this first session:

- **EM 1.5 LTS is out** — quick tour of what shipped: Landscape mode,
  CronoFilter, Document Manager, Proxy Box Creator, the standalone
  `s3dgraphy` library on PyPI, the first fully featured 3DSC for
  Blender.
- **The new [extendedmatrix.org](https://extendedmatrix.org)** —
  what changed, what's coming on the 1.6 dev line, and where the
  documentation now lives.
- **StratiGraph EU project (2025–2029)** — what's being hardened as
  shared infrastructure (governance, CIDOC-CRM / HDTO alignment,
  reference triplestore).
- **Open floor** — questions, demos, ongoing problems on participants'
  desks; people sharing what they were currently working on with EM.

The session was held informally on Microsoft Teams and was **not
recorded** by default — the meeting was a discussion-first happy hour,
not a recorded talk. If recording becomes useful for a specific
future topic, it is agreed live with the participants.
