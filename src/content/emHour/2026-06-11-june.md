The **first community happy hour** of the new EM cycle — open floor,
screen-shares welcome, no registration.

**📅 Wednesday, 11 June 2026  ·  🕓 16:00 – 17:00 CET  ·  🔗 [Join on Microsoft Teams](https://teams.microsoft.com/meet/391652615209179?p=zvw8dplEO88EhblzOS)**

What's on the agenda this first session:

- **EM 1.5 LTS is out** — quick tour of what shipped: Landscape mode,
  CronoFilter, Document Manager, Proxy Box Creator, the standalone
  `s3dgraphy` library on PyPI, the first fully featured 3DSC for
  Blender.
- **The new [extendedmatrix.org](https://extendedmatrix.org)** —
  what changed, what's coming on the 1.6 dev line, and where the
  documentation now lives.
- **StratiGraph EU project (2025–2029)** — what's being hardened as
  shared infrastructure (governance, CIDOC-CRM / HDTO alignment,
  reference triplestore).
- **Open floor** — bring questions, demos, ongoing problems on your
  desk. If you're working with EM on a real project, this is the slot
  to share progress and gotchas.

No registration needed — click the Teams link above at the start of
the meeting. **A Microsoft account is not required**: Teams opens in
the browser and asks for a display name on the join page. Please use
your real name (and an affiliation if relevant) — these are small
community sessions, knowing who's in the room is part of the
informal-but-professional tone we aim for. The session is **not**
recorded by default; if recording would be useful for a specific
topic, it's agreed live with the participants.
