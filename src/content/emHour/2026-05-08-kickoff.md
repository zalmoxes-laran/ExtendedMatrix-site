The Extended Matrix Happy Hour is back — Thursdays, 17:30 CET, an hour of
informal conversation about EM, ongoing projects, problems on people's
desks, and where the framework is heading.

The first meeting is a kick-off:

- A quick tour of EM 1.5 highlights (Landscape mode, CronoFilter, TSU).
- Where StratiGraph (the EU project) is taking the formal language.
- Open floor — bring questions, screen-shares, or just listen.

No registration needed. Hop in via the meeting link above. Sessions are
recorded only with explicit consent.

If you would like to host a future session, propose a topic via the
[Telegram group](https://t.me/UserGroupEM) or open an issue with the
`em-hour` label.
