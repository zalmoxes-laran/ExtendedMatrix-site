The **July edition** of the recurring community happy hour. Same
format as the kickoff: open floor, screen-shares welcome, no
registration.

**📅 Thursday, 9 July 2026  ·  🕓 14:00 – 15:00 CET  ·  🔗 [Join on Microsoft Teams](https://teams.microsoft.com/meet/391652615209179?p=zvw8dplEO88EhblzOS)**

Topics will be confirmed in the days before via the
[Telegram group](https://t.me/UserGroupEM). The agenda is
intentionally light — bring ongoing problems, demos and questions
from your own work; this is the slot to share progress and gotchas
with the rest of the community.

No registration needed — click the Teams link above at the start of
the meeting. **A Microsoft account is not required**: Teams opens in
the browser and asks for a display name on the join page. Please use
your real name (and an affiliation if relevant). The session is **not**
recorded by default; if recording would be useful for a specific
topic, it is agreed live with the participants.

If you would like to host a future session or propose a topic, reach
out via the
[Telegram group](https://t.me/UserGroupEM) or open an issue with the
`em-hour` label on
[github.com/zalmoxes-laran](https://github.com/zalmoxes-laran).
