After the kickoff session on 11 June, **EM Hour adopts a fixed
monthly cadence**: the **second Thursday of each month, 14:00 CET**.
Earlier announcements (and the site's previous wording) suggested a
weekly slot — that was the pre-launch plan and has now been
corrected. Monthly is what the community will actually keep up
sustainably, and it leaves room for ad-hoc topical sessions in
between when something specific comes up.

## Next session — Thursday 9 July 2026, 14:00 – 15:00 CET

The format stays the same as the June kickoff:

- Open floor, no slide deck unless a participant brings one.
- Screen shares welcome.
- "What are you working on this week?" — bring ongoing problems,
  demos, gotchas.
- Topics for the July edition will be confirmed in the days before
  via the
  [Telegram group](https://t.me/UserGroupEM).

**[Join on Microsoft Teams →](https://teams.microsoft.com/meet/391652615209179?p=zvw8dplEO88EhblzOS)**

No registration needed — click the link at the start of the
meeting. **A Microsoft account is not required**: Teams opens in
the browser and asks for a display name on the join page. Please
use your real name (and an affiliation if relevant) — these are
small community meetings where it helps to know who's in the room.

## Where the rest of the meetings calendar lives

EM Hour upcoming sessions are listed at
[extendedmatrix.org/community](https://extendedmatrix.org/community#em-hour),
together with the lightweight archive of past sessions.

The more technical **Development Meetings** (monthly, Tuesday
afternoon at 15:00 CET, exact Tuesday per session) have their own
rail on the same page; the most recent one — 23 June — is
[published with notes, decisions and action items](https://extendedmatrix.org/community#dev-meetings).
If you would like to host a future EM Hour session or propose a
topic, reach out via the
[Telegram group](https://t.me/UserGroupEM) or open an issue with
the `em-hour` label on
[github.com/zalmoxes-laran](https://github.com/zalmoxes-laran).
