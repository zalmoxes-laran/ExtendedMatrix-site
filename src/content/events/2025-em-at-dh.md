Extended Matrix participated in the **Digital Humanities 2025**
conference with a series of talks and posters. The contributions
focused on:

- the **StratiGraph European project** and its workstream;
- the maturing **CIDOC-CRM alignment** of the EM data model;
- the role of EM as a building block of the
  **European Collaborative Cloud for Cultural Heritage** (ECCCH).
