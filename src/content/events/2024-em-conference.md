The **first Extended Matrix Conference** brought together archaeologists,
heritage specialists, 3D modellers and digital humanists to look at the
state of the formal language, the framework around it, and the
projects that already use it in production.

The event served as the **user-facing counterpart of EM Bazaar** (the
developer track), focused on workflows, case studies, teaching
practices, and integration with archaeological infrastructures.
