**EM Bazaar** is the developer-focused gathering of the Extended Matrix
community: a working session where contributors meet, demo what they
are building on top of the framework, and align on the next steps for
the codebase.

It pairs naturally with **EM Conference**, which serves the user-side
of the same community.
