The foundational paper of Extended Matrix. Introduces the formal language
that turns archaeological stratigraphy into a machine-readable graph and
defines the conventions later releases extend. **Always cite this paper**
in academic work that uses Extended Matrix, regardless of the specific
version employed — see the [Citing EM](/cite/) page for the cumulative
citation rule.
